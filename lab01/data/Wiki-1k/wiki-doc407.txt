   The Dring's Horned Frog (Xenophrys dringi) is a species of
   [19]amphibian in the [20]Megophryidae family. It is found in [21]Brunei
   and [22]Malaysia. Its natural [23]habitats are subtropical or tropical
   moist [24]montanes and [25]rivers.
