Eduardo Conte

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

   Jump to: [6]navigation, [7]search

   Eduardo Conte (1946 in [8]Recife [9]January 16, [10]2003 in
   [11]Petrpolis ) was a [12]Brazilian [13]singer and [14]actor.

   He portrayed [15]Jesus Christ Superstar in Brazil in the 1970s but lost
   his life to cancer in 2003. He was married to model and actress
   [16]Betty Lago for many years.
