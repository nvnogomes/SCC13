   Dorothea of Bulgaria (Doroslava) was the daughter of [15]Ivan Sratsimir
   of Bulgaria and his wife [16]Anna of Wallachia.

   [17]Louis I, [18]King of Hungary retained her and her sister at the
   Hungarian court after her father was restored in Vidin in 1370. Later
   [19]King Louis I gave her in marriage to his cousin-in-law, ban
   [20]Tvrtko I Kotromani of Bosnia.

   In December [21]1374 in Saint Ilija (today's [22]Ilinci) near [23]id
   Dorothea married [24]Tvrtko I of Bosnia and became banness of Bosnia.
   In 1377 her husband was crowned King and she took the title of Queen of
   Bosnia. Queen Dorothea died in 1390.
