Donald Cameron, 24th Lochiel

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

   Jump to: [6]navigation, [7]search
   Donald Cameron of Lochiel with [8]Clementina Maude, Viscountess
   Hawarden, 1861.

   Donald Cameron of Lochiel (5 April 1835 30 November 1905) was a
   [9]Scottish [10]chieftain, the 24th chief ("Lochiel") of [11]Clan
   Cameron.

   He was the eldest son of [12]Donald Cameron, 23rd Lochiel, and
   succeeded his father as chief in 1858. In 1875 he married Margaret
   Montagu-Douglas-Scott, daughter of [13]Walter Montagu-Douglas-Scott,
   5th Duke of Buccleuch; the couple would have four sons, including
   [14]Donald Cameron, 25th Lochiel. From 1887 he was the [15]Lord
   Lieutenant of Inverness-shire.^[16][1]
