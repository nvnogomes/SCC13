Dollands Moor Freight Yard

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

   Jump to: [6]navigation, [7]search

   Dollands Moor Freight Yard is a railway freight yard near [8]Folkestone
   in Kent, and was purposely built in 1988 for the [9]Channel Tunnel. It
   is to the west of the [10]Cheriton Terminal, and just to the south of
   the [11]M20 Motorway.
