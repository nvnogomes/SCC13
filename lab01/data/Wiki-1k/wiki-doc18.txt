Bad title

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

   Jump to: [6]navigation, [7]search

   The requested page title is invalid. It may be empty, contain
   [8]unsupported characters, or include a non-local or incorrectly linked
   [9]interwiki prefix. You may be able to locate the desired page by
   searching for its name (with interwiki prefix, if any) in the search
   box.

   Possible causes are:
     * an attempt to follow a link to a [10]diff for a page that has since
       been deleted;
     * an attempt to load a [11]URL such as
       [12]http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/| (the | character is not
       permitted in page titles);
     * an attempt to load an URL pointing to a "non-local" interwiki page.
       For example, the URL
       [13]http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/meatball:WikiPedia will give this
       error, because the "meatball:" interwiki prefix is not marked as
       local in the interwiki table. Certain interwiki prefixes are marked
       as local in the table. For example, the URL
       [14]http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/meta:Main_page can be used to load
       [15]meta:Main_page. All interlanguage prefixes are marked as local,
       and thus URLs such as [16]http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/fr:Accueil
       will work as expected.
