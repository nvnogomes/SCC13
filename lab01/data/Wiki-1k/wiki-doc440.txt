   Acrorumohra hasseltii is a species of [21]fern in the
   [22]Dryopteridaceae family. It occurs from [23]India, through
   south-east Asia, as far as north-east [24]Queensland in [25]Australia.
   Its natural [26]habitat is subtropical or tropical moist lowland
   [27]forests. It is threatened by [28]habitat loss.
