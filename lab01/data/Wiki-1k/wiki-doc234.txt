   Dorimne Roy Desjardins

   Marie-Clara Dorimne Roy Desjardins (1858-1932) and her husband
   [9]Alphonse Desjardins were co-founders of the Caisses populaires
   Desjardins (today [10]Desjardins Group), a forerunner of [11]North
   American [12]credit unions.
