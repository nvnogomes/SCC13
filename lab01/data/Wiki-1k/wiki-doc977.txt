Eesti kullafond (Gunnar Graps)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

   Jump to: [6]navigation, [7]search

   "Eesti kullafond" (Gunnar Graps) (eng. - Estonian Golden Fund) is the
   first collection of recordings by [8]Estonian rock-musician [9]Gunnar
   Graps even released (Hitivabrik, [10]2002). It contains of 3 [11]CD's
   with the 56 best hits by different bands of Graps ([12]Mikronid,
   [13]Ornament, [14]Magnetic Band, [15]Gunnar Graps Group) and few
   solo-records (included the Estonian version of "Gethsamene" from
   "[16]Jesus Christ Superstar" [17]rock opera).
