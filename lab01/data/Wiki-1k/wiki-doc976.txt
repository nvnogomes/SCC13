   CAPTION: EAKL

   Confederation of Estonian Trade Unions
   Eesti Ametihingute Keskliit
   Founded 1917, as Central bureau of Trade Unions (Ametihingute Keskbroo)
   1927, as Confederation of Estonian Trade Unions (Eestimaa Tlishingute
   Keskliit)
   Cur. affiliation date 1990
   Members 47,500
   Country [9]Estonia
   Affiliation [10]ITUC, [11]ETUC
   Key people [12]Harri Taliga, chairman
   Office location [13]Tallinn, Estonia
   Website [14]www.eakl.ee

   The Confederation of Estonian Trade Unions is a [15]trade union centre
   in [16]Estonia. It is affiliated with the [17]International Trade Union
   Confederation, and the [18]European Trade Union Confederation.
