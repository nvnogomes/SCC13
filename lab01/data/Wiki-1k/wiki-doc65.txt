   Dmitry Sinitzyn (born [12]October 29, [13]1973) is a [14]Russian
   [15]nordic combined athlete who competed from [16]1997 to [17]2002. He
   won two bronze medals at the [18]1999 FIS Nordic World Ski
   Championships in [19]Ramsau (15 km individual and 4 x 5 km team).

   Sinitzyn also earned three individual career victories in [20]1997 (two
   individual, one sprint).
