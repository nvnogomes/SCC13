   Eastview High School (EVHS) is a public four-year [26]high school in
   [27]Apple Valley, Minnesota. It is a part of Minnesota [28]Independent
   School District 196 (Rosemount, Apple Valley and Eagan). It is
   affiliated with the [29]Minnesota State High School League (MSHSL) and
   is a member of the [30]Lake Conference. Established in 1997, it is the
   newest of six high schools serving [31]Independent School District 196.
   [32]Newsweek ranked the school in their "List of the Top High Schools
   in America for the fourth consecutive time (2000, 2002, 2004, and 2006,
   2007)."^[33][1] . Eastview has also been recognized by US News & World
   Report as one of Americas best High Schools.

[[74]edit] Facilities

   Eastview High School, District 196's fourth comprehensive high school,
   opened in September 1997. Eastview High School is located in Apple
   Valley, adjacent to 200 acres (0.81 km^2) of playing fields and
   recreational facilities. The 330,000-square-foot (31,000 m^2) facility
   is built to house 2,000 students in grades 9-12. Eastview High School's
   students have achieved high honors in academics, athletics, and the
   arts. Eastview was identified as a ten star school (highest rating) by
   the Minnesota Department of Education.
