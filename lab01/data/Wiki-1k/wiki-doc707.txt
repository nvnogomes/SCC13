Earle Cabell Federal Building and Courthouse

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

   Jump to: [6]navigation, [7]search

   [8]Coordinates: [9]324644N 964807W / 32.778867N 96.802073W / 32.778867;
   -96.802073

   The Earle Cabell Federal Building and Courthouse, named for [10]Earle
   Cabell and located in the [11]Government District of [12]downtown
   [13]Dallas, [14]Texas ([15]USA), is a large courts building that houses
   a variety of federal and state courts.

   The building houses [16]United States District Court for the Northern
   District of Texas, which exercises original jurisdiction over 100
   counties in North and West Texas, [17]United States Bankruptcy and
   Magistrate Courts and a [18]United States Attorney office.
