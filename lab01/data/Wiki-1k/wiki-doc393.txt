Dreamtime boulder

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

   Jump to: [6]navigation, [7]search

   Dreamtime is a [8]boulder problem in [9]Cresciano, [10]Switzerland. Its
   [11]first ascent (FA) was by [12]Frederic Nicole on [13]2000-[14]10-28.
   It was once considered the benchmark [15]V15. Since that time,
   Dreamtime has allegedly been chipped and is reportedly easier, having
   been downgraded to 8B+. (Nicole claims that the use of wire brushes to
   clean holds has damaged it.) Climbers [16]Malcolm Smith and [17]Dave
   Graham claim that it has always been 8B+, and Smith even doubts that it
   has been chipped.
