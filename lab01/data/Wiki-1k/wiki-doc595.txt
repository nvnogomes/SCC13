   Dzoragyugh ([14]Armenian: ; also [15]Romanized as Dzoragyukh; formerly,
   Vali Agalu; also recorded as Dvoragyukh) is a [16]town in the
   [17]Gegharkunik province of [18]Armenia. The town has a ruined church
   dating back to the 9th century, and a hermitage of the 9th century.
