Dynamo Saint Petersburg

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

   Jump to: [6]navigation, [7]search

   Dynamo Saint Petersburg is a Russian professional [8]basketball club
   that is based in [9]Saint Petersburg, [10]Russia. The club was founded
   in the year 2004 and within only a year, it won the [11]FIBA Europe
   League championship in 2005, against [12]BC Kyiv. After another year,
   the club went bankrupt in 2006.
