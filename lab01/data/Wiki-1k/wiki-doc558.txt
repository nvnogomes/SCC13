Dutch wife

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

   Jump to: [6]navigation, [7]search
   Look up [8]Dutch wife in [9]Wiktionary, the free dictionary.

   Dutch wife may refer to:
     * a long body-length pillow better known as a [10]dakimakura
     * a [11]prostitute
     * a [12]sex doll
     * a [13]hot water bottle
     * a wicker or bamboo pillow-shaped "cage" which is kept in the bed on
       hot nights to improve air circulation. See [14]Chikufujin.
