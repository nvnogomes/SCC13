   "Divine Hammer" is a song by [25]The Breeders, released as the second
   single from their album [26]Last Splash in 1993.

   An earlier, working title for the song was entitled "You Always Hang
   Around", which was recorded during the [27]Pod recording sessions. This
   version featured alternate lyrics, yet still maintained a similar song
   structure.

[[28]edit] Track listing

    1. "Divine Hammer" (Single version) - 2:42
    2. "Hoverin'" - 2:53 (Deal/Murphy)
    3. "I Can't Help It (If I'm Still in Love With You)" - 2:32 ([29]Hank
       Williams)
    4. "Do You Love Me Now Jr?" - 3:00 (Kim Deal/Kelley Deal)

   "Divine Hammer" is a different version than the LP version, and "Do You
   Love Me Now Jr?" is an alternate version of the LP version featuring
   [30]J Mascis on backing vocals.

[[31]edit] Music Video

   The music video for Divine Hammer was directed by [32]Richard Kern,
   [33]Kim Gordon, and [34]Spike Jonze.
