East Newark School District

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

   Jump to: [6]navigation, [7]search

   The East Newark School District is a community public [8]school
   district that serves students in pre-kindergarten through eighth grade
   from [9]East Newark, [10]New Jersey, [11]United States.

   For grades 9-12, public school students attend [12]Harrison High School
   in [13]Harrison, as part of a [14]sending/receiving relationship with
   the [15]Harrison Public Schools.

[[16]edit] Schools

     * East Newark Public School (grades K-8). As of the 2005-06 school
       year, the school had an enrollment of 240 students.^[17][1]
