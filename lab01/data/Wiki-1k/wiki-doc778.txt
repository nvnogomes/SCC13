Easton Grey

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

   Jump to: [6]navigation, [7]search

   Easton Grey is a [8]village in the [9]English county of [10]Wiltshire.
   It is located on the [11]B4040 between [12]Malmesbury and [13]Sherston.

   It is notable for a sixteenth century bridge over the [14]River Avon;
   overlooking the bridge is a manor house dating back to the thirteenth
   century that was a favourite resort of [15]Herbert Asquith during his
   premiership, and subsequently developed an association with the then
   [16]Prince of Wales.
