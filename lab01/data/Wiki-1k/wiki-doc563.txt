Dwight Cenac

   Dwight Cenac (born [12]March 5, [13]1983) is an [14]American [15]actor,
   [16]filmmaker, and internet personality most noted for his role as the
   modelling agent Trae Lord in the film [17]The System Within. He is
   currently making [18]Sandrima Rising, a [19]Star Wars fan film that he
   both wrote and is directing.
