Dragor River

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

     (Redirected from [6]Dragor, river)
   Jump to: [7]navigation, [8]search
   The river in the centre of Bitola.

   The River Dragor ([9]Macedonian: , Reka Dragor) is a small river
   situated in the south of the [10]Republic of Macedonia. It flows mainly
   through the city of [11]Bitola. Its [12]spring is located near
   Sapunica, on the [13]Baba Mountain. The Dragor is a right tributary
   with [14]River Crna.
