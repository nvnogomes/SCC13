   Dziemierzyce [19][demjts] is a [20]village in the administrative
   district of [21]Gmina Racawice, within [22]Miechw County, [23]Lesser
   Poland Voivodeship, in southern [24]Poland. It lies approximately
   4 kilometres (2 mi) south-west of [25]Racawice, 15 km (9 mi) south-east
   of [26]Miechw, and 34 km (21 mi) north-east of the regional capital
   [27]Krakw.^[28][1]

   The village has a population of 200.
