Doner Company

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

   Jump to: [6]navigation, [7]search

   Doner Company (formerly W.B. Doner & Co.) is an American [8]advertising
   agency [9]headquartered in [10]Southfield, Michigan. It was founded in
   1937 by Wilfred Broderick Doner. It is the largest privately-owned
   advertising agency in the United States.^[[11]citation needed] The
   Doner Company also has a [12]United Kingdom-based agency called
   [13]Doner Cardwell Hawkins Company.

   Among its clients, past and present, are:
     * [14]Mazda - the "Zoom, zoom commercial"
     * [15]Expedia - "Go With Confidence"
     * [16]May Department Stores
     * [17]Blockbuster
     * [18]Timex - "Takes a licking and keeps on ticking"
     * [19]Arby's - Oven Mitt
     * [20]Six Flags - Mr. Six
     * [21]ADT Security Services
     * [22]Cox Communications - [23]Digital Max
     * [24]Sylvan Learning Center
     * [25]Great Wolf Lodge
     * [26]AAA
     * [27]The UPS Store
     * [28]HGTV
     * [29]Zaditor
     * [30]Detroit Zoo
     * [31]PNC Financial Services
     * [32]Serta
     * [33]DuPont
     * [34]Owens Corning
     * [35]Online Vehicle Exchange
     * [36]Hotels.com
     * [37]Sherwin-Williams
     * [38]Bush Brothers and Company (Bush's Beans)
     * [39]GE
     * [40]American Kennel Club
     * [41]Kwikset
