ESP AX

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

     (Redirected from [6]ESP AX Series)
   Jump to: [7]navigation, [8]search

   The ESP AX series is a series of [9]electric guitars produced by
   [10]ESP.

   The AX Series is produced in LTD in the [11]United States and
   [12]Europe.

   Notable users of models in the AX Series include [13]Makro of
   [14]Samael, [15]Peter Scheithauer of [16]Temple of Brutality, and
   [17]Robb Flynn of [18]Machine Head.

   The design of the guitars in the AX Series is similar to that of the
   [19]B.C. Rich Warlock. All of the AX guitars feature a string-thru body
   with a [20]Tune-O-Matic Bridge. All of them also feature one volume and
   one tone control knob with a 3-way toggle with a [21]pickup formation
   of H-H.

   The series began in 2003 with only the AX-350. In 2005, the AX-350 was
   discontinued and was replaced with the AX-400, AX-250, and the AX-50.
   The AX-250 is very similar to the AX-350 except that it is made with
   [22]bolt-on neck construction instead of [23]neck-thru like its
   predecessor and the pickups are not [24]EMG HZs but instead are EMG-ESP
   LH-300's.

[[29]edit] ESP LTD AX-400

   The AX-400 features [30]set-in neck construction in a 25.5" scale. The
   [31]fingerboard features a custom tribal inlay. It has 24 extra jumbo
   frets and an Earvana compensated nut. It comes in see-through black or
   see-through red with black hardware and white binding on the neck.
     * Other Specs
          + Body Wood: [32]Mahogany
          + Neck Wood: 3-piece [33]Mahogany
          + Fingerboard Wood: [34]Rosewood
          + Pickups: 2 [35]EMG 81 active pickups
          + Bridge: [36]Tune-O-Matic with string-thru body

[[37]edit] ESP LTD AX-260

   The AX-260 features [38]neck-thru construction in a 25.5" scale. The
   [39]fingerboard features a custom tribal inlay. It has 24 extra jumbo
   frets. It comes in black with black hardware.
     * Other Specs
          + Body Wood: [40]Agathis
          + Neck Wood: 3-piece [41]Mahogany
          + Fingerboard Wood: [42]Rosewood
          + Pickups: 2 EMG-ESP LH-300
          + Bridge: [43]Tune-O-Matic with string-thru body

[[44]edit] ESP LTD AX-50

   The AX-50 features [45]bolt-on neck construction in a 25.5" scale. The
   [46]fingerboard features dot inlays with the model name on the 12th
   fret. It has 24 extra jumbo frets. It comes in silver satin and black
   satin with black hardware.
     * Other Specs
          + Body Wood: [47]Basswood
          + Neck Wood: [48]Maple
          + Fingerboard Wood: [49]Rosewood
          + Pickups: 2 ESP LH-150
          + Bridge: [50]Tune-O-Matic with string-thru body
