   Hypnotize is the fifth [58]album by [59]System of a Down and the second
   half of the [60]Mezmerize/Hypnotize [61]double album before taking
   their hiatus to work on their individual solo projects. It was released
   on [62]November 22, [63]2005, six months after the release of companion
   album [64]Mezmerize.

[[73]edit] Reception

   The album opened at #1 on the [74]Billboard 200 with 320,000 copies on
   [75]November 22, [76]2005. The album was certified gold and platinum by
   the [77]RIAA on [78]December 13, [79]2005. ^[80][1]

   [81]Entertainment Weekly (No. 851, p.100) - "[T]hey're flaunting a
   heretofore underexplored sensitive side, offsetting their trademark
   stampeding riffology with flashes of delicate lyricism." - Grade: B+

   [82]Rolling Stone (No. 988, p.117) - 3.5 out of 5 stars - "Hell
   immediately runneth over on Malakian's scoured-staccato guitars and
   Dolmayan's furious hammering."

   [83]Spin (p.62) - Ranked #20 in Spin's "40 Best Albums Of 2005."

   [84]Uncut (p.109) - 3 stars out of 5 - "System succeed through their
   skill at wielding complex, progressive heaviness in a pop context."
