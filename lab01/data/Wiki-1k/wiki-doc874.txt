Edict on the Transfer of the Capital

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

   Jump to: [6]navigation, [7]search

   Edict on the Transfer of the Capital ([8]Vietnamese:Chiu di or Thin
   chiu [9]Chinese:) is the edict written by King [10]Ly Thai To and was
   issued on the spring of [11]1010 to transfer the capital of [12]Dai Co
   Viet from [13]Hoa Lu to [14]Dai La.
   Edict on the Transfer of the Capital

[[33]edit] Meaning

   The Edict on the Transfer of the Capital is of great meaning in many
   respects. The work has been researched in terms of [34]history,
   [35]politics, [36]literature, [37]geography, [38]philosophy and so on.
