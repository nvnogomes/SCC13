Dusty Allen

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

   Jump to: [6]navigation, [7]search

   Dusty Allen ([8]August 9, [9]1972 in [10]Oklahoma City, Oklahoma), is a
   retired [11]Major League Baseball player who played [12]infielder in
   [13]2000. He would play for the [14]Detroit Tigers and [15]San Diego
   Padres.
