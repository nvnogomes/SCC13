Ed Dieckmann

   Ed Dieckmann is a former guitarist and vocalist with Dutch
   [12]rockabilly band the [13]Crocats and blues outfit Fake Brothers.

   He is the writer of two [14]Beatles books about their record releases
   in Holland: The Beatles Made In Holland (1996, [15]ISBN 90-803935-2-5)
   and The Beatles Made In Holland Too (1997, [16]ISBN 90-803935-1-7), as
   well as the International Dark Horse Records And Ring 'O Records
   Discography (2002, [17]ISBN 90-803935-3-3). An updated and extended
   version of the latter was published in 2004. Also, together with Franck
   Leenheer he published George Harrison: Dark Horse And Beyond -
   Discography 1974-2004 (2004, [18]ISBN 90-803935-4-1). In May 2007, Ed
   issued #1 of The Beatles Collectors Magazine.

   Taylor)
   02) CD Holland Billy - 16 Of The Best Dutch Rockin' Bands (1990,
   Rockhouse RockCD 9005)
   VA CD, Features Silhouet Rock (Piet Peulen) + Brand New Cadillac (Vince
   Taylor)

   VA LP, Features Tennessee Rockin' (Neale)
   04) CD Holland Billy Vol. 2 (1991, Rockhouse RockCD 9010)
   VA CD, Features Don't Go My Way (Piet Peulen)

   VA CD, Features Tennessee Rockin' (Neale)

   Features Brand New Cadillac (Vince Taylor), Tennessee Rockin' (Neale),
   Silhouet Rock (Piet Peulen) + Little Pig (Mathis/Kilgore)
   The Shakin Silhouets at the time were 'in between' guitarists, and Ed
   helped out on guitar and backing vocals on these tracks.
