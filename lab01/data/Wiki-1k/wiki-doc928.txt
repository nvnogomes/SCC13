Edward Coyne

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

   Jump to: [6]navigation, [7]search
   Eastern Suburbs Player Number: 82

   Edward Coyne was a [8]rugby league player in the [9]Australian
   competition the [10]New South Wales Rugby League (NSWRL).

   Coyne played for the [11]Eastern Suburbs club in the 1916, 1818 and
   1919 seasons.
