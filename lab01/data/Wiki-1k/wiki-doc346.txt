Dr. Rafael Ma. Moscoso National Botanical Garden

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

     (Redirected from [6]Dr. Rafael Ma. Mascoso National Botanical Garden)
   Jump to: [7]navigation, [8]search
   The main entrance to the garden.

   Dr. Rafael Ma. Moscoso National Botanical Garden (Jardin Botanico
   Nacional Dr. Rafael Ma. Moscoso) is a [9]botanical garden in the heart
   of [10]Santo Domingo. The park was founded in 1976 and was named after
   [11]Dr. Rafael Ma. Moscoso, a Dominican [12]botanist who cataloged the
   flora on the island of [13]Hispaniola^[14][1].

   The garden's symbol is a "guanito" leave ([15]Coccothrinax argentea)
   which is a palm tree found in the garden. The garden is a decentralized
   institution that reports to the Dominican Republic's Presidential
   Ministry of Management.
