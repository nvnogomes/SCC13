East London Inventors Club

   East London Inventors Club (also known as ELIC) was setup at the
   [12]University of East London (at their [13]Docklands campus) as a way
   for [14]inventors in the area to get together and share their expertise
   and resources. The club normally meets on the last Thursday of each
   month with the exception of August and December when the university is
   closed for the holidays.

   The club is known for its various achievements, in particular for their
   online inventors forum which at the time of writing has some 440
   members and over 1900 articles.
