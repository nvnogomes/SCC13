   Dolichognatha is a widespread [19]genus of [20]tropical/[21]subtropical
   [22]spiders.

[[23]edit] Species

     * [24]Dolichognatha aethiopica Tullgren, 1910 East Africa
     * [25]Dolichognatha albida (Simon, 1895) Sri Lanka, Thailand
     * [26]Dolichognatha baforti (Legendre, 1967) Congo
     * [27]Dolichognatha cygnea (Simon, 1893) Venezuela
     * [28]Dolichognatha deelemanae Smith, 2008 Borneo
     * [29]Dolichognatha ducke Lise, 1993 Brazil
     * [30]Dolichognatha erwini Brescovit & Cunha, 2001 Brazil
     * [31]Dolichognatha incanescens (Simon, 1895) Sri Lanka, Borneo, New
       Guinea, Queensland
     * [32]Dolichognatha kampa Brescovit & Cunha, 2001 Brazil
     * [33]Dolichognatha kratochvili (Lessert, 1938) Congo
     * [34]Dolichognatha lodiculafaciens (Hingston, 1932) Guyana
     * [35]Dolichognatha longiceps (Thorell, 1895) Myanmar
     * [36]Dolichognatha mandibularis (Thorell, 1894) Sumatra
     * [37]Dolichognatha mapia Brescovit & Cunha, 2001 Brazil
     * [38]Dolichognatha maturaca Lise, 1993 Brazil
     * [39]Dolichognatha minuscula (Mello-Leito, 1940) Guyana
     * [40]Dolichognatha nietneri O. P.-Cambridge, 1869 Sri Lanka
     * [41]Dolichognatha pentagona (Hentz, 1850) USA to Venezuela
     * [42]Dolichognatha petiti (Simon, 1884) Congo, Bioko
     * [43]Dolichognatha pinheiral Brescovit & Cunha, 2001 Brazil
     * [44]Dolichognatha proserpina (Mello-Leito, 1943) Brazil
     * [45]Dolichognatha quadrituberculata (Keyserling, 1883) Peru
     * [46]Dolichognatha raveni Smith, 2008 New Guinea, Queensland
     * [47]Dolichognatha richardi (Marples, 1955) Samoa
     * [48]Dolichognatha spinosa (Petrunkevitch, 1939) Panama
     * [49]Dolichognatha tigrina Simon, 1893 West Indies, northern South
       America
     * [50]Dolichognatha umbrophila Tanikawa, 1991 Taiwan, Okinawa Islands
