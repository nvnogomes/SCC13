Easthouses

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

   Jump to: [6]navigation, [7]search
   Derelict industrial building on former colliery land

   Easthouses is a town in [8]Midlothian, [9]Scotland, lying just outside
   [10]Dalkeith. It forms the northern extension of the settlement of
   [11]Mayfield, with which it is closely associated.

   Primarily based on public housing, Easthouses developed in the 1920s to
   accommodate miners for the nearby [12]Easthouses Colliery, many of whom
   relocated from the west of Scotland. The settlement was significantly
   extended in the 1950s. Employment has diversified since the closure of
   the mines, with many of the population now commuting to Edinburgh.

   The Easthouses Parish Church (built 1954) is of minor architectural
   interest.
