Doug Potter

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

   Jump to: [6]navigation, [7]search

   Doug Potter is a creator who has worked in the [8]comics industry. In
   recognition of his work, he was nominated for the [9]Harvey Award for
   Best New Talent in 1990^[10][1].
