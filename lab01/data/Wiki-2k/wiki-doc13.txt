Distance Education Council

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

   Jump to: [6]navigation, [7]search

   Distance Education Council (DEC) is an organisation based in [8]New
   Delhi, [9]India responsible for the promotion and coordination of the
   open university and [10]distance education system and for determination
   of its standards in India. The Council was constituted under the
   [11]Indira Gandhi National Open University Act (1985).
