   Don McLean is an album by [25]American singer-songwriter [26]Don
   McLean, released in 1972. It was reissued by [27]BGO Records in 1996.

[[28]edit] Track listing

   All tracks composed by Don McLean; except where indicated
    1. "Dreidel"
    2. "Bronco Bill's Lament"
    3. "Oh, My What a Shame"
    4. "If We Try"
    5. "The More You Pay"
    6. "Narcisissima"
    7. "Falling Through Time"
    8. "On the Amazon" ([29]Vivian Ellis, [30]Clifford Grey)
    9. "Birthday Song"
   10. "The Pride Parade"
