   The Dusky Fulvetta (Schoeniparus brunnea) is a species of [19]bird in
   the [20]Timaliidae family. It is found in [21]China, [22]India, and
   [23]Taiwan. Its natural [24]habitats are temperate [25]forests and
   subtropical or tropical moist lowland forests.
