Ebelyakh Bay

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

   Jump to: [6]navigation, [7]search
   Location of Ebelyakh Bay at the eastern end of the Laptev Sea.

   The Ebelyakh Bay or Ebelyakhskaya Bay (Russian: Ebelyakhskaya Guba) is
   a bay in the [8]Laptev Sea. It is located east of the [9]Yana Bay
   region and southwest of the [10]Laptev Strait.

   The bay is 36 Km in width and wide open to the northwest.

   This bay is in a vast region of wetlands. [11]Ozero Bustakh, the
   largest lake in the neighboring area, lies 37 Km inland from the shores
   of the bay. Owing to is northerly location the Ebelyakh Bay is covered
   with ice most of the year.

   The Ebelyakh Bay and the area surrounding it belong to the [12]Sakha
   Republic (Yakutia) administrative division of the [13]Russian
   Federation.
