   Eddy Jorge Garabito (born [16]December 2, [17]1976 in [18]Manrreza,
   [19]Dominican Republic) is a [20]second baseman for the [21]St. George
   Roadrunners of the [22]Golden Baseball League. He played in [23]Major
   League Baseball for the [24]Colorado Rockies and in the minor leagues
   for the [25]Baltimore Orioles.
