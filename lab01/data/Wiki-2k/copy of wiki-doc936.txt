Edward H. Bersoff

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

   Jump to: [6]navigation, [7]search

   Edward H. Bersoff is the President, CEO and founder of BTG, Inc.

   Prior to founding BTG in 1982, Bersoff was President of CTEC, Inc.
   Previously, as an officer in the U.S. Army, he was assigned to the
   [8]NASA Electronics Research Center in Cambridge, Massachusetts. He
   taught mathematics at [9]New York University, [10]Kingsborough
   Community College in New York, [11]Northeastern University, [12]Boston
   University and [13]American University.

   He serves as Chairman of the Board of Directors of the Virginia
   Economic Development Partnership, and serves by appointment of Virginia
   Governor George Allen on the Joint Subcommittee to Study Capital Access
   and Business Financing.

   Bersoff holds A.B., M.S. and Ph.D. degrees in mathematics from [14]New
   York University.
