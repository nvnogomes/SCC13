Domenico Bettini

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

   Jump to: [6]navigation, [7]search

   Domenico Bettini (born 1644) was an [8]Italian painter of the
   [9]Baroque. He was born in [10]Florence. He was first a pupil of
   [11]Jacopo Vignali, but afterwards went to Rome, and became a pupil of
   [12]Mario Nuzzi. He painted [13]still lifes with fruit, flowers, birds,
   and fish.
