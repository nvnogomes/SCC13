   Eastern Province is one of the three provinces of [15]Sierra Leone. It
   covers an area of 15,553 km and has a population of 1,187,532 (2004
   [16]census). Its [17]capital and [18]administrative center is
   [19]Kenema.

[[20]edit] Districts

   The province is divided into 3 districts.
     * [21]Kailahun District, capital [22]Kailahun
     * [23]Kenema District, capital [24]Kenema
     * [25]Kono District, capital [26]Koidu Town

[[27]edit] Borders

   Eastern Province has the following borders:
     * [28]Nzrkor Region, [29]Guinea: northeast
     * [30]Lofa County, [31]Liberia: east
     * [32]Gbarpolu County, [33]Liberia: southeast
     * [34]Grand Cape Mount County, [35]Liberia: south
     * [36]Southern Province: west
     * [37]Northern Province: northwest

   Eastern Province is the only region of Sierra Leone without any
   shoreline.
