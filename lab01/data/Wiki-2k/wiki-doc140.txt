   Dominica Public Service Union
   Founded [8]1938
   Country [9]Dominica
   Affiliation [10]CTUC
   Key people [11]Sonia Williams, president
   [12]Thomas Letang, general secretary
   Office location [13]Roseau, Dominica

   The Dominica Public Service Union is a [14]trade union in [15]Dominica.
   It was originally founded in [16]1938 as the Dominica Civil Service
   Association.
