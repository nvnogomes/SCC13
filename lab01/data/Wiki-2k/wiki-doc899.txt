Edoardo de Martin

   Jump to: [6]navigation, [7]search

             [8]Bobsleigh
          World Championships
   Silver [9]1967 Alpe d'Huez Two-man

   Edoardo de Martin is an [10]Italian [11]bobsledder who competed in the
   late 1960s. He won a silver medal in the two-man event at the [12]1967
   FIBT World Championships in [13]Alpe d'Huez.
