   CAPTION: Dosamp

   Screenshot of Dosamp.
   [8]Developed by [9]Nullsoft
       [10]OS      [11]DOS
      [12]Type     [13]media player
     [14]License   [15]freeware

   Dosamp is a software audio [16]MP3 player made by [17]Nullsoft for
   [18]DOS operating systems. The project got dropped soon in favor of
   WinAMP product, thus has only historical relevance by now. There is
   however an up-to-date DOS audio player called [19]MPXPLAY available,
   unrelated to DOSamp.
