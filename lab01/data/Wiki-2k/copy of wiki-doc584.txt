Dynowatt

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

   Jump to: [6]navigation, [7]search

   Dynowatt, headquartered in [12]Dublin, Ohio is an electricity supplier
   to [13]residential customers from [14]Dallas to [15]Houston to Corpus
   Christi to Odessa and most places in-between.

   Dynowatt now offers 100% green [16]renewable electricity generated by
   Texas [17]wind power.

   In 2007, [18]Accent Energy acquired the assets of Dynowatt.
