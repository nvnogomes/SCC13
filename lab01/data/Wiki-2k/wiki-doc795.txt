   Echephron is the name of three characters in [13]Greek mythology.
     * Echephron, son of [14]Nestor and [15]Eurydice (or
       [16]Anaxibia).^[17][1]^[18][2]

     * Echephron, a son of [19]Priam, king of [20]Troy.^[21][3]

     * Echephron, son of [22]Heracles and [23]Psophis, daughter of
       [24]Eryx, a [25]Sicilian despot. He changed the name of the city
       [26]Phegia (the old [27]Erymanthus) to [28]Psophis.^[29][4]
