Don Juan (Brecht)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

   Jump to: [6]navigation, [7]search

   Don Juan is an adaptation by the twentieth-century German dramatist
   [8]Bertolt Brecht of a seventeenth-century French play by [9]Molire.

   [12]expanding it.
