   Double Or Nothing is the second solo album by [28]hip-hop artist
   [29]Erick Sermon. It was well-received and moderately popular with two
   charting singles, "Bomdigi" and "Welcome". It has guest appearances
   from a young [30]Jazze Pha as well as a young [31]Def Squad.
