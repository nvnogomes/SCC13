   Drake & Josh Go Hollywood is a TV movie starring [32]Drake Bell as
   Drake Parker and [33]Josh Peck as Josh Nichols from the popular
   [34]Nickelodeon television series [35]Drake & Josh. The movie first
   aired on [36]January 6, [37]2006 and was released on DVD on [38]January
   31, 2006. The movie premiered on Nickelodeon Israel on [39]September
   19, [40]Nickelodeon UK on [41]October 26 2006, and in Canada on [42]YTV
   [43]November 19, 2006. Additionally, it aired in Australia on
   [44]Nickelodeon on [45]December 16, 2006. According to [46]TV Guide,
   the movie was the highest rated program on all of cable during its
   opening week.

[[61]edit] Trivia

   [62]Lists of miscellaneous information should be avoided. Please
   [63]relocate any relevant information into appropriate sections or
   articles. (April 2008)
     * Professional skateboarder [64]Tony Hawk makes a brief cameo in the
       movie.
     * In the Movie both [65]Drake and [66]Megan are seen playing a
       [67]Sony PSP
     * The video Josh was watching on his computer of Drake and his band
       was actually when Drake Bell guest starred in [68]Zoey 101.
     * In this film, the [69]laugh track was removed. Not only that but
       the film is in widescreen, it has movie camera angles, which is the
       same way Zoey 101 is shown on TV. The movie also doesn't feature
       the theme song.
     * One of the guys that worked for TRL said to meet him at Sunset
       Studios at 4:00 for sound check. Sunset Studios is where shows such
       as Drake & Josh and Zoey 101 are shot.
     * When the camera is facing the front of the windshield to show the
       characters, the road and background is a [70]greenscreen.
     * In the airport, Josh watches a channel called "CCN", a parody of
       CNN.
     * When Megan's "Pacific Air" plane lands in LA, you can see the
       "[71]American Airlines" logo on the tail.
     * When Drake and Josh's "Pacific Air" plane lands in LA, you can see
       the "[72]Southwest Airlines" logo on the tail.
     * In "Drake and Josh Go Hollywood", Drake and Josh drive Megan to the
       airport in what looks like Drake's "muscle" Mustang. The next year
       during "My Dinner With Bobo" in Season 4, Drake tells Walter about
       this awesome retro Mustang. Josh adds that it has over 200,000
       miles on it. Prior to that scene, Walter tells Drake that he had
       his own car which Drake replied "which I lost".
     * Megan is seen reading a magazine called "Persons." This is most
       likely a parody of People magazine.

[[76]edit] Sequel

     * [77]Drake & Josh: Really Big Shrimp aired on Nickelodeon on
       [78]August 3, [79]2007. It is an hour-length episode that shows

       [80]Merry Christmas, Drake & Josh.
