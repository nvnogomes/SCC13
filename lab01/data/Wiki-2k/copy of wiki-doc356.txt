DRA

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

     (Redirected from [6]Dra)
   Jump to: [7]navigation, [8]search

   DRA is a three-letter acronym that may refer to:
     * [9]Defence Research Agency, a former executive agency of the United
       Kingdom
     * [10]Democratic Republic of Armenia, a short-lived Armenian republic
       in the South Caucasus (1918-1920)
     * [11]Democratic Republic of Afghanistan, the communist regime that
       ruled Afghanistan between 1978 and 1992
     * [12]Deutscher Reichsausschuss fr Olympische Spiele (DRA or DRAfOS)
       "German Imperial Commission for Olympic Games", a former German
       Olympic Sports organization.
     * Deutsches Rundfunkarchiv, see [13]German Broadcasting Archive
     * [14]Dielectric Resonator Antenna
     * [15]Digital rectal exam
     * [16]Don't Run Away, a program that educates elementary and middle
       school students about the consequences and alternatives of running
       away
     * [17]Downregulated-in-adenoma, an anion exchanger
     * [18]Driver reminder appliance (British Rail)
