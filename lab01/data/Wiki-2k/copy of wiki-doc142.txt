Dominican Church

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

   Jump to: [6]navigation, [7]search

   Dominican Church is the name of a number of churches belonging to the
   Dominicans, e.g.:
     * [8]Dominican Church (Vienna), Austria
     * [9]Dominican Church (Krems), Austria
     * [10]Dominican Church (Vc), Hungary
     * [11]Dominican Church (Krakow), Poland
     * [12]Dominican Church (Bansk tiavnica), Slovakia
     * [13]Dominican Church (Koice), Slovakia
