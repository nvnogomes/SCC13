   Dru (born Andrew Grange), is a [22]Canadian, [23]R&B and [24]Pop
   [25]singer-songwriter, [26]record producer and [27]performer who was
   the lead singer of R&B group [28]In Essence and is currently embarking
   on a solo career. As of summer 2008 he was in the studio completing
   final touches on his album, "The One" to be released in the fall of
   2008. In September 2008, he released a single called Seasons.
