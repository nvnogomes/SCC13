   Dustin Cooper (born 8 May 1981) is an [15]Australian [16]rugby league
   player who currently plays as either a centre or fullback for the
   [17]Cronulla Sharks. He previously played for the [18]Melbourne Storm
   where he made his first grade debut in 2003. He moved to [19]Newcastle
   in 2005 and in 2007 he moved to [20]Cronulla Sharks.
