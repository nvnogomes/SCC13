   The Durrs Amphitheatre ([13]Albanian: Amfiteatri i Durrsit) is a large
   [14]Roman amphitheatre in the city of [15]Durrs, [16]Albania. Built in
   the second century AD, it is located in the centre of the city and is
   only half unearthed. The Durrs Amphitheatre is the largest amphitheatre
   in the [17]Balkans, once having a capacity of 20,000 people. It was
   discovered in the late 1900's and today has become a popular tourist
   attraction.
