Dyment Falls (Hamilton, Ontario)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

     (Redirected from [6]Dyment Falls)
   Jump to: [7]navigation, [8]search

          See also: [9]List of waterfalls in Hamilton, Ontario

   CAPTION: Dyment Falls

     Location   [10]Hamilton, Ontario
     [11]Type   Overhang Ribbon
   Total height 15.7 m (52 ft)
   Total width  4 m (13 ft)
   Watercourse  Spencer tributary

   Dyment Falls is a 15.7 metre high overhang ribbon [12]waterfall found
   in [13]Flamborough, [14]Hamilton, Ontario, [15]Canada.^[16][1]

   Nearby attractions include Dyment's Farm, Rock Chapel Sanctuary,
   [17]Borer's Falls, Borer's Falls Conservation Area, [18]Sydenham Falls,
   [19]Spencer Gorge / Webster's Falls Conservation Area.^[20][2]

[[25]edit] Directions

   From [26]Highway 403 take the [27]Highway 6 North ([28]Guelph) exit and
   travel approximately 3 kilometres to [29]Highway 5 East and then turn
   left onto Sydenham Road. Park on your left at the lookout point near
   the [30]Escarpment brow. Walk back up Sydenham Road and follow the
   Bruce Trail. The waterfall will be on your left.
