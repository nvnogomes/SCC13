List of Hungarian rulers

     (Redirected from [6]Duke of Hungary)

   This is a list of all rulers of Hungary since rpd.

   See [9]Heads of state of Hungary for a list of post-1918 presidents.

[[10]edit] Rise of a Hungarian state (8951000) and the [11]Kingdom of Hungary
(10001918)

   [12]rpds [13]lmos c.858-c.895 lmos was the first Hungarian leader,
   father of rpd. He strengthened the alliance between the other six
   proto-[14]Magyar tribal leaders.
   [15]rpds [16]rpd c.895c.907 rpd is said to have led the
   proto-[17]Magyars into [18]Central Europe. According to the dual system
   of rulership (based on the similar Khazar rulership method), he was the
   actual ruler leader alongside of [19]Kurszn as religious leader.
   The rulers during the first half of the 10th century are very much
   disputed, as the Hungarian nation consisted of several tribes led by
   various leaders. Most frequently proposed are:

   [125]Szapolyai [126]John I Zpolya
   (Szapolyai Jnos) 15261540 claimed the throne with support of Hungarian
   nobles and later the [127]Ottoman Sultan.
   Szapolyai [128]John II Sigismund Zpolya (Szapolyai Jnos Zsigmond)
   1540-1570 son of John Zpolya, electus rex Hungariae, first under the
   guardianship of his mother [129]Isabella Jagieo and [130]Frater Gyrgy,
   renounced his royal claim in 1570 in favour of [131]Maximilian, but
   remained [132]Prince of Transylvania and parts of Hungary until his
   death in 1571.
   Hungary was effectively split into three parts: [133]Royal Hungary in
   the north and west, [134]Ottoman Hungary in the south, and the
   [135]Principality of Transylvania in the east. The following, until
   1699, gives the rulers of "Royal Hungary".
