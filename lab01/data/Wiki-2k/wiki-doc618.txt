EBUL

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

   Jump to: [6]navigation, [7]search

   EBUL is a [8]record label belonging to [9]Pete Waterman Ltd and
   Jive/Zomba.

   EBUL was established by [10]Pete Waterman from the ashes of his
   [11]Eastern Bloc Records and [12]Unity record labels. A deal was soon
   struck with Jive's Steve Jenkins and early successes include the pop
   band [13]Steps, and dance outfit Atlantic Ocean ("Waterfall").

   As of [14]2004, EBUL is a going concern and recent acts include Lauren
   Waterworth and Pop!
