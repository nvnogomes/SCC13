Edgar Russell

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

     (Redirected from [6]Edgar Hugo Degenaart Russell)
   Jump to: [7]navigation, [8]search

   Edgar Hughes Degenaart Russell (24 October 1890 31 March 1963) was an
   [9]Australian politician. Born at Booleroo Centre in [10]South
   Australia, he was educated at public schools, where he became a bank
   clerk and manager, and later a private accountant. In 1943, he was
   elected to the [11]Australian House of Representatives as the [12]Labor
   member for [13]Grey, defeating sitting [14]United Australia Party
   member [15]Albert Badman. He held the seat until his death in
   1963.^[16][1]
