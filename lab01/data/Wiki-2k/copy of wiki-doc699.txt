Earl of Ardglass

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

   Jump to: [6]navigation, [7]search

   The title Earl of Ardglass (pronounced "Ar-glass") was created in the
   [8]Peerage of Ireland in 1645. The Earl held the subsidiary titles of
   [9]Baron Cromwell and [10]Viscount Lecale (1624). All three titles
   became extinct in 1687.

[[11]edit] Barons Cromwell (1540)

     * [12]Gregory Cromwell, 1st Baron Cromwell (1514 1551)
     * [13]Henry Cromwell, 2nd Baron Cromwell (1538 1592)
     * [14]Edward Cromwell, 3rd Baron Cromwell (1559 1607)
     * [15]Thomas Cromwell, 4th Baron Cromwell (created Earl of Ardglass
       in 1645)

[[16]edit] Earls of Ardglass (1645)

     * [17]Thomas Cromwell, 1st Earl of Ardglass (15941653)
     * [18]Wingfield Cromwell, 2nd Earl of Ardglass (16221668)
     * [19]Thomas Cromwell, 3rd Earl of Ardglass (16531682)
     * [20]Vere Essex Cromwell, 4th Earl of Ardglass (16231687)
