   Batted: Right Threw: Right
   MLB debut

   Last MLB appearance
   [19]October 3, [20]1993 for the [21]New York Yankees
   Career statistics
   [22]Win-loss record     1-1
   [23]Strikeouts     20
   [24]Earned run average     4.46
   Teams
     * [25]New York Yankees (1993)

   Domingo Jean Luisa is a former [26]Major League Baseball pitcher who
   played in 10 games for the [27]New York Yankees in [28]1993. He was
   originally signed by the [29]Chicago White Sox in [30]1989 and traded
   to the Yankees in [31]1992 as part of a package for [32]Steve Sax.
   After his brief tenure with the Yanks, he continued to play minor
   league ball until [33]2002, when he retired.
