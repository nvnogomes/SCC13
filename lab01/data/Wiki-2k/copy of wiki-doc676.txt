EXC code

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

   Jump to: [6]navigation, [7]search

   EXC^[8][1] is a [9]condensed matter physics [10]many-body theory
   [11]software package implementing the [12]Bethe-Salpeter equation in
   frequency-reciprocal space and on a [13]plane wave basis set. Its
   purpose is to calculate, [14]ab initio, dielectric and optical
   properties, like [15]absorption, [16]reflectivity, [17]refraction
   index, electron and X-ray [18]energy loss, for a large variety of
   systems, ranging from bulk systems, surfaces, to clusters or atoms. It
   is distributed under the GNU/GPL license.
