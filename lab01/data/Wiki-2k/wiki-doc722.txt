   Earth to the Dandy Warhols is the sixth official studio album by
   [37]alternative rock band [38]The Dandy Warhols, released for download
   and streaming play on [39]19 May, 2008.^[[40]citation needed] The LP &
   CD versions of the album were released 18 August 2008. It is their
   first recording after leaving [41]Capitol Records. On [42]May 5th, the
   band gave away the song "The World The People Together (Come On)"
   (later re-named to "The World Come On") as a free download on their
   website along with announcing that the album was available to play on
   over 10,000 AMI Jukeboxes.

[[50]edit] Notes

     * [51]Dire Straits lead singer and guitarist [52]Mark Knopfler and
       [53]Tom Petty & The Heartbreakers guitarist [54]Mike Campbell guest
       on the song "Love Song."
     * The sound at the last second of "Musee D'Nougat" is echoed at the
       beginning of "The World Come On" making the album playable on
       repeat without any noticeable gap.
     * So far videos for Mission Control, Mis Amigos, Now You Love Me, The
       legend of the Last of the Outlaw Truckers, The World Come On, Talk
       Radio and Welcome to the Third World have been filmed.
     * The Legend of the Last of the Outlaw Truckers was featured in an
       episode of french-Canadian television series [55]Les Invincibles on
       February 18, 2009.
