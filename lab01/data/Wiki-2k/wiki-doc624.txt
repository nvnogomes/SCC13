ECP

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

   Jump to: [6]navigation, [7]search

   ECP is an abbreviation for a number of terms, including
     * [8]East Capitol Pensionne, a very nice and affordable place to stay
       while in Cebu, Philippines
     * [9]East Coast Parkway, a major highway in the south-east of the
       island-state of [10]Singapore
     * [11]Eastman Color Positive, a [12]photographic processing system
     * [13]cole Centrale Paris, a French [14]university
     * [15]Effective Core Potential, a term in [16]computational chemistry
     * [17]Electrical Code of Practice
     * [18]Electrochemical corrosion potential
     * [19]Electrochemical potential
     * [20]Electronic Communications in Probability, a scientific journal
     * Electronic Couch Potato, after the STB monitoring solution.
       Performs monitoring on television signals by emulating a human
       changing channels on the STB.
     * [21]Electronically controlled pneumatic railway brakes.
     * [22]Emergency Care Practitioner, a type of Paramedic/Nurse
     * [23]Emergency contraceptive pill, a type of [24]contraception also
       known as morning-after pill
     * [25]Emerging Capital Partners, a private equity firm
     * [26]Empty category principle, a syntactic constraint in theoretical
       linguistics
     * [27]Engineering Change Proposal, a term used in Air Force contracts
     * [28]English Collective of Prostitutes, a feminist group
     * [29]Enterprise Content Publishing, solutions that includes a
       control on contents and publications for the enterprise enviorement
     * [30]Entry control point
     * [31]Eosinophil Cationic Protein, a pore building and
       [32]RNAse-functioning molecule synthesised by [33]eosinophils
     * [34]Equipping Christians for the Public-square Centre
     * [35]Escadrilles canadiennes de plaisance, a Canadian volunteer
       association that teaches boating safety courses
     * [36]ESRI Conservation Program, a technology grants program for
       nonprofits run by the Environmental Systems Research Institute
     * [37]European Committee for the Prevention of Torture
     * Excessive [38]Crossposting, way of [39]Usenet spam. Also known as
       Velveeta.
     * [40]Executive Cellular Processor, a part of [41]CDMA architecture
     * [42]Explorers Club of Pittsburgh, an all-volunteer outdoor
       adventure organization specializing in rock climbing and
       mountaineering
     * [43]Exposed Conductive Parts (See: [44]Earthing system)
     * [45]Extended capability port, a type of [46]parallel port used in
       computers
     * [47]Extended Care Physicians
     * [48]External CounterPulsation (See: [49]EECP)
     * [50]Extraordinary Candidacy Project, credentials openly
       gay/lesbian/bisexual/transgender seminarians, candidates, ordained
       and commissioned ministers who are preparing for professional
       vocations in independent Lutheran parishes and congregations of the
       Evangelical Lutheran Church in America (ELCA).
     * Extreme Corporal Punishment, a hardcore punk band featured in the
       film [51]SLC Punk!. Produced one album and disbanded.
     * [52]Eye care professional
