   DreamCatcher Interactive Inc. (a.k.a. DreamCatcher Games) is a
   [24]Toronto, [25]Ontario, [26]Canada-based publisher of [27]video
   games. In 2002, most of the assets and development teams of [28]Cryo
   Interactive were absorbed by DreamCatcher Interactive, forming the base
   for DreamCatcher Europe.

   DreamCatcher is best known for publishing the [29]first-person shooter
   [30]Painkiller, as well as the [31]point-and-click [32]adventure game
   [33]The Crystal Key. In October 2005, DreamCatcher released the game
   [34]Cold War, developed by [35]Mindware Studios, [36]Emergency Fire
   Response in 2003 and the [37]Ship Sim in 2006.

   On [38]November 4, [39]2006, [40]JoWooD Productions announced the
   acquisition of DreamCatcher Games, in efforts to move to the North
   American gaming markets.^[41][1]
