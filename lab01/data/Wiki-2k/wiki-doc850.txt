Edelmiro Amante

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

     (Redirected from [6]Edelmiro A. Amante)
   Jump to: [7]navigation, [8]search

   Edelmiro A. Amante, Sr. (born April 21, 1933 in [9]Butuan City), is a
   [10]Filipino politician.

[[11]edit] Education

   Amante attended [12]Ampayon Elementary School, then [13]Mindanao
   Institute, where he graduated from high school as a [14]salutatorian.
   He then studied at [15]Silliman University, where he received an
   Associate in Arts degree in 1954, then a Bachelor of Laws in 1958.

[[16]edit] Career

   Amante started his career as a [17]barrio Lieutenant in [18]Ampayon,
   [19]Butuan City. He held that position for only one year, 1959, before
   becoming a [20]Councilor, from 1960 to 1964. He represented [21]Agusan
   del Norte at the [22]1973 Constitutional Convention. He had also
   represented [23]Agusan del Norte as an Assemblyman of the defunct
   [24]Batasang Pambansa from 1978 to 1984. Since 1984, he has been
   elected to four terms to the [25]House of Representatives representing
   the [26]2nd District of Agusan del Norte. He was most recently elected
   in 2007, succeeding his daughter Angelica.

   Amante was a close ally of [27]President [28]Fidel Ramos, under whose
   administration he served as the [29]Executive Secretary from 1992 to
   1993. He currently is a member of the [30]KAMPI party.
