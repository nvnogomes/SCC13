   The Eden Foundation is a [12]Sweden-based charitable foundation
   focusing on utilization of useful plant species.

   Since 1987, Eden has been running a project in Tanout, in the
   [13]Zinder Department of [14]Niger, just south of the [15]Sahara
   desert, helping the farmers and their families achieve a sustainable
   life with the means available to them. Eden's solution was to bring
   them trees and bushes that could grow naturally in this dry area and
   give food, even in times of need. Eden has served more than 2500
   households in the area.
