   East Coast Wrestling Association (ECWA) is an [11]American
   [12]professional wrestling promotion founded by Jim Kettner on
   [13]August 27, [14]1967. Over the past 15 years the ECWA has become one
   of the most respected and visible independent wrestling companies in
   the United States. It is home to the annual [15]Super 8 Tournament, one
   of the top independent wrestling tournaments in the country. The
   promotion's shows run out of the Greater Newark Boys and Girls Club in
   [16]Newark, Delaware.
