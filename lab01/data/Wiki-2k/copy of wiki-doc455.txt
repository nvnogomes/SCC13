   Duca can refer to:
     * [11]DUCA Financial Services Credit Union Ltd. a [12]credit union
       serving the [13]greater Toronto Area in [14]Canada
     * [15]Michael Duca, current Catholic [16]Bishop of Shreveport
     * a Romanesque title of [17]duke, notably in Italian
     * a family name, notably in [18]Romanian; famous members include:
          + two [19]Voivodes of Moldavia:
               o [20]Gheorghe Duca (1665-1666, 1668-1672 and 1678-1684);
                 also Voivode of [21]Walachia 1673-1678
               o [22]Constantin Duca (1693-1695 and 1700-1703)
          + [23]Ion Duca, [24]Prime Minister of Romania from 14 November
            to 30 December, 1933
