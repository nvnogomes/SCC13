Edward Littleton, 3rd Baron Littleton

   Edward George Percy Littleton (3rd [13]Baron Hatherton) (1842-1930); he
   married Charlotte Louisa Rowley (d. 1923) in 1867. They had 9 known
   children:-
     * Edward Charles Powley Littleton (1868-1944).
     * Margaret Louisa Littleton (1869-1945).
     * Algernon Joshua Percy Littleton (1871-1951).
     * Charles Christopher Josceline Littleton b. 1872.
     * Hyacinthe Frances Littleton (1874-1951).
     * Susan Helen Littleton b. 1877.
     * William Hugh Littleton (1882-1956).
     * Mary Cecilia Littleton b. 1884.
     * Edith Modwena Littleton b. 1890.
