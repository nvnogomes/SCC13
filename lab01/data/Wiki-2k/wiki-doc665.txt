ESL (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

   Jump to: [6]navigation, [7]search

   ESL is a common abbreviation for English as a Second Language, see
   [11]English language learning and teaching.

   ESL may also refer to:

[[12]edit] Companies

     * [13]ESL ElectroScience, a materials manufacturing company based in
       Pennsylvania
     * [14]ESL Incorporated, a former technology company in the United
       States
     * [15]ESL Music, a record label
     * ESL Investments, a company founded by [16]Edward Lampert

[[17]edit] Technology concepts

     * [18]Electronic Shelf Label, a system used by retailers
     * [19]Electronic system level, an electronic design methodology
     * [20]Electrostatic loudspeaker
     * [21]Enterprise Service Layer, a term used in service oriented
       architecture (SOA)
     * [22]Equivalent series inductance, a concept in electronics
     * Equivalent static loads also know as ESLM (Equivalent static load
       method), method for optimization of systems subject to dynamic
       loads

[[23]edit] Other meanings

     * [24]Electronic Sports League, a computer gaming league
     * [25]Estonian Sign Language
     * [26]Super League (Europe), the premier rugby league competition in
       Europe
