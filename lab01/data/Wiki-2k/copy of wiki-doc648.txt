       Founded     1999
       Members     37,000
       Country     [8]Norway
     Affiliation   [9]LO
     Key people    [10]Hans Felix, president
   Office location [11]Oslo, Norway
       Website     [12]www.elogit.no

   EL & IT Forbundet is a [13]trade union in [14]Norway. It was formed by
   the 1999 merger of the [15]Electricians and Power Station Workers, and
   the [16]Data and Telecommunications Workers.

   EL & IT Forbundet has a membership of over 37,000 and is affiliated
   with the [17]Norwegian Confederation of Trade Unions (LO).
