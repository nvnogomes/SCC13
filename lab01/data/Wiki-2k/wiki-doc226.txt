   Anduzedoras oxyrhynchus is the only [19]species in the [20]genus
   Anduzedoras of the [21]catfish ([22]order Siluriformes) [23]family
   [24]Doradidae.^[25][1] This species originates from the [26]Rio Negro
   and upper [27]Orinoco River basins and reaches a length of about
   32 centimetres (13 in).^[28][2]
