      The village (dark red) and the statistical district (light green) of
                               Driehuizen in the municipality of Schermer.

   Driehuizen ([12]5235N 448E / 52.583N 4.8E / 52.583; 4.8) is a village
   in the [13]Dutch province of [14]North Holland. It is a part of the
   municipality of [15]Schermer, and lies about 8 km south of [16]Alkmaar.

   The statistical area "Driehuizen", which also can include the
   surrounding countryside, has a population of around 180.^[17][1]
