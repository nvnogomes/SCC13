   Bystrzyca Valley Landscape Park
   Park Krajobrazowy Dolina Bystrzycy
   [9]IUCN Category V (Protected Landscape/Seascape)
   Location [10]Lower Silesian Voivodeship

   Bystrzyca Valley Landscape Park (Park Krajobrazowy Dolina Bystrzycy) is
   a [11]protected area ([12]Landscape Park) in south-western [13]Poland.

   The Park lies within [14]Lower Silesian Voivodeship.
