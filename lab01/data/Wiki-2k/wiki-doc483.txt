House of Broglie

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

     (Redirected from [6]Duke de Broglie)
   Jump to: [7]navigation, [8]search
   Coat of Arms of the House of Broglie

   The House of Broglie ([9]French: Maison de Broglie; [10]pronounced
   [11]De Broglie.ogg [12][d bj] ([13]help[14]info)) is the name of a
   noble French family, originally [15]Piedmontese, who emigrated to
   [16]France in the year 1643. The family patriarch, [17]Franois-Marie
   created for himself the title of [18]Comte de Broglie. After already
   having distinguished himself as a soldier, and died, as a
   lieutenant-general, at the siege of Valenza on the 2 July 1656.

   His son, Victor-Maurice, comte de Broglie (16471727), served under
   Cond, Turenne and other great commanders of the age of Louis XIV,
   becoming marechal de camp in 1676, lieutenant-general in 1688, and
   finally marshal of France in 1724.

   The title Duc de Broglie was a [19]French peerage belonging to a family
   of [20]Piedmontese origin, which emigrated to [21]France in the year
   1643. The family counts among its members three [22]marshals of France,
   a [23]Nobel Prize laureate, and several members of the [24]Institut de
   France.

   His grandson, [25]Franois-Marie, was made duc de Broglie and a peer of
   France in 1742.

   Junior members of the family bear the title Prince de Broglie.

[[35]edit] Ducs de Broglie

     * [36]Franois-Marie, 1st duc de Broglie (16711745), marshal of France
       and governor of [37]Strasbourg
     * [38]Victor-Franois, 2nd duc de Broglie (17181804), marshal of
       France
     * [39]Victor, 3rd duc de Broglie (17851870), statesman and diplomat,
       member of the [40]Acadmie franaise
     * [41]Albert, 4th duc de Broglie (18211901), politician and writer,
       member of the [42]Acadmie franaise
     * [43]Victor, 5th duc de Broglie (18461906)
     * [44]Maurice, 6th duc de Broglie (18751960), physicist, member of
       the [45]Acadmie franaise and the [46]Acadmie des sciences
     * [47]Louis, 7th duc de Broglie (18921987), physicist and [48]Nobel
       laureate, member of the [49]Acadmie franaise and the [50]Acadmie
       des sciences
     * [51]Victor-Franois, 8th duc de Broglie (born 1949)
