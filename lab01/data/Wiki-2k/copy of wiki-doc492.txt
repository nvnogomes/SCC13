Dulwich and Sydenham Hill Golf Club

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

   Jump to: [6]navigation, [7]search

   [8]Coordinates: [9]512616N 00417W / 51.437696N 0.071389W / 51.437696;
   -0.071389

   Dulwich and Sydenham Hill Golf Club is a [10]private members club since
   1894. Overlooking [11]Dulwich College and with a backdrop of the
   [12]Sydenham Hill and [13]Dulwich woods. From the club house there are
   [14]panoramic views across the whole of [15]London.
