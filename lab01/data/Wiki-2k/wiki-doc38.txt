Division Street, Sheffield

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

   Jump to: [6]navigation, [7]search
   For other uses, see [8]Division Street (disambiguation).

   Division Street is a street and shopping area located in the
   [9]Devonshire Quarter of [10]Sheffield, [11]England. It is home to many
   small independent shops, a few large ones such as [12]Starbucks Coffee
   and [13]SPAR, and a few local pubs such as the [14]Frog & Parrot, as
   well as a Lloyd's No1 bar. It runs parallel to West Street in the west
   of the city.
