   Dusty Trails is the self-titled debut album by the American music duo
   [22]Dusty Trails. It was released in 2001 on [23]Atlantic Records.

    5. "Roll The Dice"
    6. "St. Tropez"
    7. "Unhand Me You Wretch"
    8. "They May Call Me A Dreamer"
    9. "Fool For A Country Tune"
   10. "Regrets In Bordertown"
   11. "Order Coffee"
   12. "Conga Style"
   13. "Caught in a Dream"
