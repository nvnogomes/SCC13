Edmund Czaplicki

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

   Jump to: [6]navigation, [7]search

   Edmund Czaplicki ([8]October 30, [9]1904 1940) was a [10]Polish [11]ice
   hockey player who competed in the [12]1928 Winter Olympics.

   He was born in [13]Warsaw. He also died in [14]Warsaw under unknown
   circumstances.

   In 1928 he participated with the [15]Polish ice hockey team in the
   [16]Olympic tournament.
