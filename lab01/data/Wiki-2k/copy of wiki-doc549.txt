Dsseldorf Open

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

     (Redirected from [6]Dusseldorf Open)
   Jump to: [7]navigation, [8]search

   The Dsseldorf Open was a women's professional [9]tennis tournament held
   for one year, in 1973, in [10]Dsseldorf, [11]West Germany (now
   [12]Germany). Its one year on the [13]WTA Tour saw a home player,
   [14]Helga Masthoff, triumphing in the women's singles, whilst she and
   [15]Heide Orth reached a women's doubles final that was never played.
