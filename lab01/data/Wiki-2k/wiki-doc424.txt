Drugget

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

   Jump to: [6]navigation, [7]search

   Formerly, a drugget was a sort of cheap [8]stuff, very thin and narrow,
   usually made of [9]wool, or half wool and half silk or linen; it may
   have been corded or plain, and was usually plain. The term is now
   applied to a coarse fabric having a cotton warp and a wool filling,
   used for rugs, tablecloths, etc.
