                    Bruce Hensel
   Occupation [14]Television actor, [15]journalist

   Dr. Bruce Hensel is an [16]Emmy Award-winning medical, health and
   science reporter for [17]KNBC, a television station in [18]Los Angeles.
   He had previously acted in [19]Death Wish 4: The Crackdown and the
   1980s TV [20]soap opera [21]Capitol.

   Hensel was a guest on the hit radio show [22]Loveline. He is not to be
   confused with [23]Dr. Bruce Heischober, who frequently sat in for
   [24]Drew Pinsky and was called also Dr. Bruce (or sometimes Dr. Spaz)
   by [25]Adam Carolla.
