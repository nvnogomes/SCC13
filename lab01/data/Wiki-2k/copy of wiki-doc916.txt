   Born Eduardo Jos Caldera Cranshaw

   Eduardo Jos Caldera Cranshaw better known as Edward'O (born in
   [12]Managua, [13]Nicaragua) is an [14]astroanalyst and co-host of
   [15]Telemundo's dating show, [16]12 Corazones.^[17][1]

[[22]edit] Early life

   Edward'O was born in Managua, Nicaragua and later moved to [23]Los
   Angeles, [24]California. He graduated in [25]Latin American studies and
   possesses a post-graduate in [26]Spiritual psychology.^[27][2]

   Edward'O studies varies religions such as [28]Buddhism, [29]Hinduism
   and the religions of the Native Americans among many others. He has
   dedicated over 30 years to [30]astrology and [31]esotericism^[32][3]

[[33]edit] Predictions

   Some of Edward'O predictions include the^[34][2]
     * Victory of President George Bush (which one-HW or W?)
     * [35]Northridge earthquake in [36]Los Angeles
     * [37]Loma Prieta earthquake in [38]San Francisco
     * Death of [39]Adn Snchez
