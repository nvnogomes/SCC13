   Downy Hempnettle is a plant, Galeopsis segetum, belonging to the family
   [18]Labiatae. It grows as a weed in arable ground throughout
   [19]Europe. Although superficially resembling the [20]Stinging nettle
   it is of a different family and does not sting.
