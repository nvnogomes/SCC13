Edward River

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

   Jump to: [6]navigation, [7]search
   For the river in Queensland, Australia, see [8]Edward River
   (Queensland).
   The Edward River at Deniliquin

   The Edward River is a river in the south west [9]Riverina region of
   [10]New South Wales, [11]Australia. The river is an [12]anabranch of
   the [13]Murray River, the longest river in Australia.

   The river starts at Picnic Point, as a result of the bottleneck created
   in the Murray by the [14]Cadell fault, travelling north west through
   [15]River red gum forest before reaching [16]Deniliquin. It then joins
   the [17]Wakool River before reentering the Murray.
