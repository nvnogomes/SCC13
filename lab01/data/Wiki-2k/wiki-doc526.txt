   Durahim Jamaluddin (born [23]February 13, [24]1981) is a [25]Malaysian
   [26]football player. He is the former member of Malaysia national team.

   He previously playing for [27]Perlis FA and won the 2005 [28]Malaysia
   Super League. With Malaysia, he made his only appearance against
   [29]Singapore in 2005 international matches.
