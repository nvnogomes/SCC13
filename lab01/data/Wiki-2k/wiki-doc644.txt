   EJHG (Volume 14, Issue 7 ([9]July 2006))

   The European Journal of Human Genetics is an official monthly [10]human
   genetics [11]publication. It is published by the [12]British [13]Nature
   Publishing Group, the publisher and owner of the well-known journal
   [14]Nature.

[[15]edit] Key areas

   EJHG covers many areas in the human genetics field ^[16][1]:
     * [17]Congenital disorder
     * [18]Hereditary cancer
     * [19]Monogenic disorder
     * [20]Gene mapping
     * [21]Genetic variation
     * [22]genome
     * [23]Animal models
