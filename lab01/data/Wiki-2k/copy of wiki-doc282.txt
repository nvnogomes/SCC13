   Stephen Douglas Brady (born [22]November 23, [23]1969 in
   [24]Jacksonville, Illinois) is a retired [25]Major League Baseball
   [26]second baseman. He played during one season at the major league
   level for the [27]Chicago White Sox. He was drafted by the White Sox in
   the 12th round of the [28]1991 amateur draft. Brady played his first
   professional season with their Class-A (Short Season) [29]Utica Blue
   Sox in [30]1991, and his last with the White Sox's Triple-A
   [31]Nashville Sounds in [32]1997.
