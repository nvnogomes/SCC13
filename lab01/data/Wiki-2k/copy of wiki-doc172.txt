Don Riddell

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

   Jump to: [6]navigation, [7]search

   Don Riddell (born [8]7 September [9]1972 in [10]Edinburgh, [11]United
   Kingdom) is a [12]Scottish-born [13]English [14]sports journalist and
   fill-in news anchor. He is currently one of four [15]London-based
   anchors of [16]CNN's World Sport. He formerly hosted CNN's Living Golf
   and is a regular correspondent on CNN Today, a European morning
   programme.

   Riddell joined CNN in 2002. He had previously worked at the [17]London
   News Network, where he worked as a sports presenter for the [18]London
   Tonight programme. He started his career in regional British
   television.

[[19]edit] Education

   Riddell attended the [20]University of Leeds and graduated with a
   [21]Bachelor's degree with honours in Public Media/Communication and
   Cultural Studies.
