Estonian Lutheran Association of Peace

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

     (Redirected from [6]Eesti luterlik rahuuhendus)
   Jump to: [7]navigation, [8]search

   The Estonian Lutheran Association of Peace (Estonian: Eesti Luterlik
   Rahuhendus) is a [9]conservative laestadians organization in
   [10]Estonia. It has one Association of Peace in [11]Tartu. It does
   mission work in [12]Tallinn, Tartu and Southeast Estonia. It has sister
   organizations in [13]Finland, [14]Sweden and [15]North America.
