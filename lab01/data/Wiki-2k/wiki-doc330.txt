Dr. Cecilio Putong National High School

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

   Jump to: [6]navigation, [7]search

   The Dr. Cecilio Putong National High School, originally the Bohol
   National High School or BNHS; also Bohol High, is a [8]public,
   [9]secondary [10]educational institution at [11]Tagbilaran City,
   [12]Bohol, [13]Philippines. It is located along Carlos P. Garcia or CPG
   Avenue.
