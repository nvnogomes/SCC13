                                  Home ice       [8]Eisarena Ludains

   EHC St. Moritz is a [13]Swiss [14]ice hockey team.

          Founded: 1918
          Home arena: [15]Eisarena Ludains (capacity 800)
          Swiss Championships won: 3 (1922, 1923, 1928)
          Nationalliga B Championships won: 1 (1954)
