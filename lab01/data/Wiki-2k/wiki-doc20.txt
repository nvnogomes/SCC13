District of Alberta

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

   Jump to: [6]navigation, [7]search

   The District of Alberta was one of four [8]districts of the Northwest
   Territories created in 1882. It was styled the Alberta Provisional
   District to distinguish it from the [9]District of Keewatin which had a
   more autonomous relationship from the NWT administration. The District
   of Alberta would ultimately form the southern portion of the
   [10]Province of Alberta when the latter was created in 1905.
