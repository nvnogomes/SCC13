   Edgar Yakovlevich Gess ([21]Russian: ) (born [22]March 14, [23]1954 in
   [24]Taboshary) is a retired [25]Soviet football player and a current
   [26]German manager. He is an [27]ethnic German and emigrated to Germany
   in 1989.

[[28]edit] Honours

     * [29]Soviet Top League winner: 1979.

[[30]edit] International career

   Gess played his only game for [31]USSR on [32]September 5, [33]1979 in
   a friendly against [34]East Germany.
