     (Redirected from [6]Dorde Mili)

   ore Mili is [9]Yugoslav professional [10]football player and manager.

   He played for [11]Red Star Belgrade during the 1966-67 season before
   moving to [12]Turkey to play for [13]Adanaspor and [14]Beikta in
   1970ies and later he became manager for two seasons at Adanaspor. In
   1980 he was in charge of Beikta until 1983. For ore Mili young talents
   were extremely important and he continued to look for new comers. These
   are the few he signed while he was in Beikta.
