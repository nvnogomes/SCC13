Dsseldorf-Stockum

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

     (Redirected from [6]Dusseldorf-Stockum)
   Jump to: [7]navigation, [8]search

   Stockum is an urban borough of [9]Dsseldorf. It is located north of
   [10]Unterrath and [11]Golzheim, east of [12]Kaiserswerth, west of
   [13]Lohausen and the [14]Dsseldorf Airport, south of [15]Kalkum.

   Stockum has an area of 6.70 km and 4,894 inhabitants. In opposide to
   the neighboring Kaiserswerth there is no interesting history about
   Stockum. Stockum became a part of Dsseldorf in 1909.

   In 1971 the [16]Dsseldorf Exhibition Center went to Stockum. It has 18
   halls and an exhibition area of about 200,000 m. There is a huge park
   (Nordpark) in Stockum, too. In that park there is an aqua zoo and a
   Japanese garden. North of the exhibition center there is the new
   multifunctional sports arena of Dsseldorf, the [17]LTU Arena.
