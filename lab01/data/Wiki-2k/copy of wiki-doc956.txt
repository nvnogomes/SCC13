Edward Roberts (athlete)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

   Jump to: [6]navigation, [7]search

   Edward Reese Roberts ([8]October 4, [9]1892 November, 1969) was an
   [10]American [11]track and field athlete who competed in the [12]1920
   Summer Olympics.

   He was born in [13]Avon, Illinois and died in [14]Opa-locka, Florida.

   In 1920 he finished seventh in the [15]56 pound weight throw
   competition.
