Dope Sheet

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

   Jump to: [6]navigation, [7]search

   Dope sheet can refer to:
     * A [8]dope sheet - a planning sheet for [9]animators, similar to a
       [10]storyboard. The term is nowadays also used for sheets
       describing the content of TV programmes with the help of thumbnail
       pictures from various scenes.
     * A [11]scratch sheet - a publication listing betting odds on horses
       participating in [12]horse racing.
     * [13]The Dope Sheet - the game program for professional [14]American
       football team the [15]Green Bay Packers.
