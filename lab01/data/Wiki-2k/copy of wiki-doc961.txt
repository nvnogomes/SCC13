Edward William Wynne Pendarves

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

   Jump to: [6]navigation, [7]search

   Edward William Wynne Pendarves (1775 [8]26 June [9]1853) was [10]Member
   of Parliament (MP) for [11]West Cornwall from the creation of the
   Constituency on [12]19 December [13]1832 until the year of his
   death.^[14][1]

   He was on the Committee of Management of the [15]South Western Railway
   (UK) in 1836.^[16][2] He was one of the proprietors of the
   [17]University of London, who requisitioned a special general meeting
   in 1831, to appoint a Select Committee to investigate the lack of
   progress with the project.^[18][3]
