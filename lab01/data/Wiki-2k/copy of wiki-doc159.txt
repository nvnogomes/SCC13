   Don't Shoot Me I'm Only the Piano Player is the sixth [35]studio album
   by [36]British [37]singer/songwriter [38]Elton John.

   This was Elton's second straight #1 album in America and yielded his
   first #1 single there - "[39]Crocodile Rock." According to writer
   [40]Philip Norman, Elton's friend, Groucho Marx jokingly pointed his
   index fingers at Elton John during a get together in California as if
   holding a pair of six-shooters. Elton John put up his hands and said,
   "Don't shoot me, I'm only the piano player," so naming the album. Along
   with "Crocodile Rock" Don't Shoot Me... contained more songs with a pop
   flavour, including "Elderberry Wine" (the [41]b-side of "Crocodile
   Rock"), "I'm Gonna Be a Teenage Idol", and "Teacher, I Need You." Many
   long-time fans however, lamented this change, wishing he had stayed
   true to his original roots of simple piano ballads which were the
   staple on most of his earlier [42]albums.^[[43]citation needed]

    2. "Jack Rabbit" 1:49

   When MCA Records re-issued this album on cassette tape in the 1980s,
   "Midnight Creeper" and "High Flying Bird" were swapped in the album
   running order.

   "I'm Gonna Be a Teenage Idol" was written for/about [61]Marc Bolan.

[[63]edit] Personnel

   DANIEL Elton John: electric piano & 'flute' Mellotron; [64]Dee Murray:
   bass; [65]Nigel Olsson: drums & maraccas; Ken Scott: A.R.P.
   synthesizer; [66]Davey Johnstone: acoustic guitar & banjo.

   Teacher I Need You Elton: piano & Mellotron; Dee: bass; Nigel: drums;
   Davey: acoustic guitar; Dee, Nigel & Davey: backing vocals.

   ELDERBERRY WINE Elton: piano; Dee: bass; Nigel: drums; Davey: electric
   guitar; Gus: brass arrangement; Jacques Bolognesei: trombone; Ivan
   Jullien: trumpet; Jean-Louis Chautemps & Alain Hatot: saxophones.

   Blues For My Baby And Me Elton: piano; Dee: bass; Nigel: drums; Davey:
   acoustic & electric guitars & sitar; [67]Paul Buckmaster: orchestral
   arrangement.

   Midnight Creeper Elton: electric piano; Dee: bass; Nigel: drums; Davey:
   electric guitar; Gus: brass arrangement; Jacques Bolognesei: trombone;
   Ivan Jullien: trumpet; Jean-Louis Chautemps & Alain Hatot: saxophones.

   Have Mercy On The Criminal Elton: piano; Dee: bass; Nigel: drums;
   Davey: electric guitars; Paul Buckmaster: orchestral arrangement.

   I'm Gonna Be A Teenage Idol Elton: piano & Lelsie piano; Dee: bass;
   Nigel: drums; Davey: electric & acoustic guitars; Dee, Nigel & Davey:
   backing vocals; Gus: brass arrangement; Jacques Bolognesei: trombone;
   Ivan Jullien: trumpet; Jean-Louis Chautemps & Alain Hatot: saxophones.

   Texan Love Song Davey: acoustic guitar & mandolin; Dee: bass; Nigel:
   drums; Elton: harmonium.

   CROCODILE ROCK Elton: piano & Farfisa organ; Dee: bass; Nigel: drums;
   Davey: electric guitar.

   High Flying Bird Elton: piano; Dee: bass; Nigel: drums; Davey: Leslie &
   acoustic guitars; Dee, Nigel & Davey: backing vocals.
