Eastland (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

   Jump to: [6]navigation, [7]search

   Eastland may refer to:
     * The [8]Eastland, a passenger ship that sank on the Chicago River in
       1915
     * [9]James Eastland, U.S. Senator from Mississippi
     * [10]James Eastland (Texas politician)
     * The [11]Eastland Shopping Centre, located in Ringwood, Victoria,
       Australia.
     * [12]Eastland Mall, any of several shopping malls in various
       locations in the U.S.
     * [13]Eastland, Charlotte, North Carolina
     * [14]Eastland, Texas and [15]Eastland County, Texas, of which
       Eastland is county seat
