Engineer Research and Development Center

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

     (Redirected from [6]ERDC)
   Jump to: [7]navigation, [8]search

   The Engineer Research and Development Center or ERDC is a [9]United
   States government funded [10]military base located at [11]Vicksburg,
   Mississippi. The base was set up after the 1927 [12]flood disaster of
   the [13]Mississippi River. The base is staffed by the [14]United States
   Army Corps of Engineers. With half a billion dollars of funding, their
   job is to research methods of flood control and prevention, along with
   other [15]waterways [16]technology and [17]engineering.

[[18]edit] List of ERDC facilities

     * [19]Coastal and Hydraulics Lab, [20]Vicksburg, Mississippi
     * [21]Cold Regions Research and Engineering Lab, [22]Hanover, New
       Hampshire
     * [23]Construction Engineering Research Lab, [24]Champaign, Illinois
     * [25]Environmental Lab, [26]Vicksburg, Mississippi
     * [27]Geotechnical and Structures Lab, [28]Vicksburg, Mississippi
     * [29]Information Technology Lab, [30]Vicksburg, Mississippi
     * [31]Topographic Engineering Center, [32]Alexandria, Virginia
