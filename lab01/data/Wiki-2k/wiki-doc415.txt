   Drops of Jupiter is [28]Train's second [29]album, released in 2001. The
   album's title is derived from "[30]Drops of Jupiter (Tell Me)", its
   first single which was very popular and won the [31]Grammy Award for
   Best Rock Song.

   As an album, Drops of Jupiter is difficult to place within a single
   genre, though many in the industry put the album in the Adult
   Contemporary Market. Indicative of Train's style, the album contains
   elements of [32]country, [33]rock, and [34]indie rock. Besides "Drops
   of Jupiter (Tell Me)," two other singles were released from this album:
   "She's On Fire" and "Something More," both of which achieved modest
   success on the Adult Top 40 chart.

   It has since been certified 2x Platinum in the United States and is
   their best selling album to date.
