Edward Octavian Cuthbert

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

     (Redirected from [6]Edouard-Octavian Cuthbert)

   Edward Octavian Cuthbert
   Source: Library and Archives Canada

   Edward Octavian Cuthbert ([9]December 3, [10]1826 1891) was a
   [11]Quebec politician of independent means. He represented [12]Berthier
   in the [13]Canadian House of Commons as a [14]Conservative member from
   1875 to 1887.

   He was born in [15]Berthier, [16]Lower Canada in 1826, the son of
   [17]James Cuthbert, and educated at Chambly College. He served as mayor
   of Berthier. Cuthbert was also president of the County Agricultural
   Society. He was elected to the House of Commons in an 1875 by-election
   held after the sitting member was named to the Senate.
